Our Story

From farm to family, naturally.

Mercysweet Foods was born from a simple question: why do Nigerian households have to choose between flavour and health? Why should “processed food” always mean chemicals?

Founded by Akinboye Anuoluwapo at the Technology Incubation Centre in Ilorin, we built a processing system that starts with the best local tomatoes and Nigerian honey — and does nothing except make them last longer, naturally.

Every year, millions of tons of tomatoes rot on Nigerian farms. We turned that waste into a mission: process more, waste less, and feed more families with food they can genuinely trust.

1
🌾 Farm — Locally Sourced
Fresh tomatoes and honey from Nigerian smallholder farmers. No imports. No middlemen.
2
⚙️ Processing — Zero Chemicals
Carefully processed at our Ilorin facility. No preservatives. Science-backed natural methods.
3
🏠 Your Table — Pure & Fresh
Real food with 12–18 months of natural shelf life, tasting exactly as food should.
